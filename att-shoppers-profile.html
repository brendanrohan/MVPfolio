Overview

I led the design of AT&T's Shoppers Profile system, creating a unified customer data platform that enabled personalized retail experiences across in-store and digital touchpoints.

The Challenge

AT&T retail associates lacked visibility into customer preferences, purchase history, and service interactions, leading to generic conversations and missed upsell opportunities.
